FAQ

What is TSH?

TSH stands for Thyroid Stimulating Hormone. It is a hormone produced by the pituitary gland that regulates the thyroid gland's production of thyroid hormones T3 and T4.

What is Free T4?

Free T4 is a blood test that measures the level of free thyroxine (T4) in the bloodstream. T4 is one of the hormones produced by the thyroid gland that helps regulate the body's metabolism.

What is Free T3?

Free T3 is a blood test that measures the level of free triiodothyronine (T3) in the bloodstream. T3 is one of the hormones produced by the thyroid gland that helps regulate the body's metabolism.
